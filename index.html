Lost and Found

Please use this website in order to report and claim lost items found around the school.

  1. Do not claim items that are not yours
  2. If an item is particularly expensive (electronics, car keys, etc.), we will confirm proof of ownership before exchange
  3. If reporting a lost item, please include a picture if available
  4. All unclaimed items will be donated to local shelters at the end of the semester
